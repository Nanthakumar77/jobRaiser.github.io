JobRaiser ATS

JobRiser ATS is one of the most simplified and easy to use software product that helps Direct Employers to manage Talent Acquisition Process and Recruitment Consultants to manage the complete recruitment and billing process

JobRaiser ATS is designed for small and medium enterprises who look for a simple to use and highly stable to manage talent acquisition or end-to-end recruitment consulting business process

Key Features of JobRaiser ATS

Download Resumes from Job Portals.
Recruitment Workflow Automation.
Job Posting to Job Portals and Social Media.
Tracker sheet generation, MIS and online Dashboards.
Integrated to MS Outlook® to Parse Resumes in bulk.
Mobile App to track transactions and Status updation
